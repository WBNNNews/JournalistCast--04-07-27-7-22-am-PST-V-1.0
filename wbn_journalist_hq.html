Article Framework
WBN editorial standards · Every section, every format, every rule
1 — Title & Byline
Required · Always first
→
Title format: 🌍 WBN Breaking News – [DATE]: [Primary Signal Headline]
→
Headline rule: Market-aware, outcome-focused, sharp. No passive voice. Max 15 words.
→
Byline: By WBN Global News Desk | WBN News Subscribe (https://wbn.digital?fpr=george65) | [DATE]
2 — Opening & At A Glance
Required · Sets the signal
→
Opening paragraph: 1–2 sentences max. Global impact + economic signal. No fluff.
→
At A Glance: Exactly 5 bullet points · 4–6 words each · Top signals across all regions
3 — Regional Sections
Required · In this order
→
Order: 🍁 Canada → 🦅 United States → 🌍 Africa → 🌐 International
→
Each story: Headline / Date / Source / Summary (1 sentence) / Why It Matters (1 sentence, business lens)
→
Minimum: 5 stories per region. Business and economic relevance only.
4 — Watch List
Required · 8–12 items
→
Title format: 👀 Watch List – Developing Stories
→
Format: [Story] — [Region] — [Source]
→
Stories that did not make the main sections but carry forward signal value for business.
5 — Why It All Matters
Required · Signal analysis
→
Open with: “What we are seeing is not a single story—it’s a system-level shift.”
→
Then: 3–4 short lines (no bullets). Cover: Markets · Energy · Governments · Global positioning
→
Tone: Strategic, signal-driven. Not descriptive. Not journalistic. Business intelligence only.
6 — Footer, Tags, Scroll & Meta
Required · End of every brief
→
Footer: WBN Global News Desk · World Business News · Subscribe link · newsdesk@wbnn.news
→
Tags: Start with #Breaking News · No space between # and word · Space between words
→
Breaking Scroll: 🔴 Exactly 20 items · 4–6 words each · No periods · Separated by “ * ”
→
Meta: Title ≤56 chars · Description ≤140 chars · At very end of article
